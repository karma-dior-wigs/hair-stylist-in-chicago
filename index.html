The 6-Step Booking Process

Step 3: Consultation – A full consultation is conducted to determine your desired look, hair density, length, lace type, and whether you are booking:

• Wig + Install
Step 4: Wig Processing – Each unit is professionally processed for you, which may include:

Step 6: Install Day – Your wig is installed with precision, secure placement, and detailed baby hair sculpting for a flawless finish.
