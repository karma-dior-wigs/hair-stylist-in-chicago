The Karma Dior Wig Experience

1. Start Your Consultation

Begin by reaching out directly to Karma. Your consultation starts with an email reply where we discuss your wig goals, custom wig order, or wig + install details. Click below to get started:

karmadiorsuites@gmail.com

2. Wig Selection & Custom Order

During your consultation, we’ll go over lace type, length, density, parting style, color options, and whether you’re providing your own unit or ordering through Karma. This step ensures your wig matches your features and desired look.

3. Deposit to Secure Your Appointment

A $50 non-refundable deposit is required to lock in your wig order and/or install date. Accepted payment methods include Cash App, Apple Pay, Zelle, and PayPal. Payment instructions are provided during your consultation.

4. Wig Prep & Customization

Before your appointment, your wig is professionally shampooed, conditioned, brushed, combed, and detangled. Lace is customized, the hairline is shaped, and baby hairs are mapped for a natural finish. Your unit arrives fresh and ready for a flawless install.

5. Install Day

Installs are clean, secure, and tailored to your features. Baby hairs are laid with precision, blends are seamless, and your look is finished with optional lashes or hair accessories for a polished result.

6. Maintenance & Follow-Up

After your install, Karma provides guidance on upkeep, reinstall timing, and wig care. Maintenance services include glue refreshes, lace cleaning, restyling, and full wig revamps.
